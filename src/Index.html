How to fill the schedule
click to toggle
1
Load your schedule
Choose your player name and click Load. This brings back your saved availability before you start editing.
2
Paint available time
Click or drag on the grid to mark when you can play. Green means selected, red means unselected, and greyed out slots are locked for another campaign or reserved time, so they cannot be changed.
3
Remove mistakes quickly
On desktop, right click and drag to remove slots. On touch devices, switch between the Add and Remove buttons.
4
Save before leaving
If the banner says Unsaved changes, click Save. Use presets, fill day, copy previous day, undo, and redo to speed things up.
Use Apply (Add) to keep your current picks and add preset hours. Use Apply (Replace) to rebuild your schedule from preset rules. After saving, open Campaign availability to see what sessions became playable.
